    A note on hormonal birth control. Since you are currently using hormonal contraception, it is worth knowing that it works by suppressing your natural hormones and replacing them with synthetic ones. You do not get a real period on it, just a withdrawal bleed. If it is not medically necessary for you, it may be worth slowly and gently exploring what coming off it could look like, with proper support. Your natural cycle holds a lot of information about your health, and reconnecting with it can be genuinely life-changing.
