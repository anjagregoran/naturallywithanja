The Rhythm Within You

Your Cycle Has Four Seasons

Just like nature, your body flows through four phases every month. When you learn to live with them instead of against them, everything, your energy, mood, skin, and body, begins to feel easier.

Menstrual
Days 3-7
Your Winter
Your Winter
Rest deeply. Warm, iron-rich foods. Energy is low and inward. Gentle movement only. Your body is working hard, honor the need to slow down and be gentle with yourself.
Follicular
Days 6-13
Your Spring
Your Spring
Fresh energy rises. Move a little more. Eat light and vibrant. Creativity, planning, and new beginnings feel natural. A beautiful time to start things.
Ovulatory
Days 14-17
Your Summer
Your Summer
You shine and connect. Confidence, magnetism, and social energy peak, this is your radiant, outward time. Cooling foods and sunshine suit you now. Move freely.
Luteal
Days 18-28
Your Autumn
Your Autumn
Wind down and nourish. Energy turns inward, a time to tidy, prepare, and care for yourself deeply. Grounding foods and magnesium. Slow your movement as your period nears.
